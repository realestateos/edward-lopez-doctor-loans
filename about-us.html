About Edward Lopez

Arizona's trusted physician mortgage specialist helping doctors achieve homeownership since 2008

Your Arizona Physician Mortgage Expert

Hello, I'm Edward Lopez, and I've dedicated my career to helping medical professionals achieve the dream of homeownership here in Arizona. With over 15 years of experience specializing exclusively in physician mortgage loans, I understand the unique financial challenges and opportunities that come with a career in medicine.

I know that doctors, residents, and fellows face a distinct set of circumstances—significant student loan debt, delayed earnings during training, but exceptional future income potential. Traditional mortgage lenders often don't understand this profile, which is why I focus exclusively on physician loan programs that are designed specifically for medical professionals like you.

Since 2008, I've helped over 500 doctors, residents, and medical professionals across Arizona secure financing for their homes. From first-year residents at Banner University Medical Center to established attending physicians at Mayo Clinic Arizona, I've guided medical professionals through every step of the home buying process.

Why I Specialize in Physician Mortgages

I chose to specialize in physician mortgage loans because I believe medical professionals deserve better financing options. After seeing countless doctors struggle to qualify for conventional mortgages due to high student loan debt or lack of employment history during residency, I knew there had to be a better way.

Physician mortgage loans recognize your earning potential and offer benefits that simply aren't available with traditional loans—0% down payment, no PMI, and the ability to qualify using your employment contract. These programs changed the game for medical professionals, and I'm proud to be Arizona's leading expert in this specialized field.
