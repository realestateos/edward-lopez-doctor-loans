About Edward Lopez

Your trusted physician mortgage specialist in Arizona

Your Physician & Advanced Medical Professional Loan Specialist

Edward Lopez

NMLS ID: 598677

Edward Lopez is a dedicated mortgage professional with over 15 years of experience specializing in physician mortgage loans. As a Senior Mortgage Loan Officer with BOK Financial, Edward has helped hundreds of medical professionals across Arizona achieve their dream of homeownership.

Understanding the unique financial challenges faced by physicians—including high student loan debt, limited work history during residency, and the need for flexible qualification criteria—Edward has developed expertise in navigating the complexities of physician mortgage programs.

Based in Mesa, Arizona, Edward serves medical professionals throughout the Phoenix metropolitan area, including Scottsdale, Tempe, Chandler, and Gilbert. His deep knowledge of the local real estate market combined with specialized physician loan products makes him the go-to mortgage specialist for Arizona doctors.

Professional Background

Edward began his career in mortgage lending after graduating from Arizona State University with a degree in Finance. Recognizing the unique needs of the medical community, he specialized in physician mortgage programs early in his career.

Over the years, Edward has built strong relationships with the top physician mortgage lenders in the country, giving his clients access to the most competitive rates and terms available. His expertise extends beyond traditional physician loans to include specialized programs for residents, fellows, and attending physicians.

Edward is licensed to originate mortgages in Arizona and maintains the highest standards of professional ethics and customer service. He believes in educating his clients throughout the mortgage process, ensuring they understand their options and feel confident in their decisions.
