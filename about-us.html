Community Mortgage Banker | Physician Lending Specialist

Edward Lopez is a Community Mortgage Banker with BOK Financial, specializing in physician mortgage lending throughout Arizona. With over 20 years of experience in the financial services industry, Edward finds immense fulfillment in discovering the perfect solutions for his clients.

He is dedicated to understanding each client's unique needs and crafting the ideal mortgage plan for their future. By building strong relationships and getting to know his clients personally, Edward ensures he can serve them best. Whether you're buying your first home, a vacation property, or an investment property, Edward has the expertise to help.

  • Personal Service: Available throughout the entire process, from initial consultation to closing day.
