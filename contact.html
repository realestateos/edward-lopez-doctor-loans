Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get pre-approved?

In most cases, we can provide a pre-approval letter within 24-48 hours of receiving your completed application and documentation.

Can I use my employment contract as proof of income?

Yes! One of the key benefits of physician mortgages is that we can use your signed employment contract as proof of income, even if you haven't started working yet.

Do I need a down payment?

Our physician mortgage programs offer up to 100% financing, meaning no down payment is required. This allows you to preserve your savings for other expenses.

Will my student loans affect my approval?

Student loans deferred for more than 12 months from closing are typically not counted in your debt-to-income ratio, making it easier to qualify.

What credit score do I need?

While requirements vary, most physician mortgage programs require a minimum credit score of 700. However, we review each application individually.

Can I buy an investment property?

Physician mortgages are designed for primary residences. However, we offer other loan programs for investment properties - contact us to discuss your options.
